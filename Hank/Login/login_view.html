UWA FAQ Chat System

The purpose of this application is to provide an easy-to-use platform for anyone seeking information about the University of Western Australia. With the chat system.

FAQ

Users can easily receive help from a chatbot or representative about answers to their inquiries about the university.

History

Users are able to create an account to track their chat history, which allows them to quickly and easily access previous conversations for reference.

Prompt Responses

Users can receive personalized and timely responses to their inquiries, helping them to make informed decisions about their education goals.
